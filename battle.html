◂ Return to Ancient Greece
── A mini strategy game ──

Macedonia vs Persia

Persia (AI)
Macedonia (you)
Rules of engagement
▸ 6×6 board. Three units per side. Eliminate the enemy to win.
▸ Each turn, move or attack with each of your units, then End turn.
▸ Movement: Infantry and General move 2 tiles, Cavalry moves 4 tiles (diagonals count).
▸ Attacking always deals full damage to the target.
▸ If the target survives, it strikes back for half its damage — except the Persian Archer.
▸ The Archer takes no return damage when firing from 2 tiles away, but takes full return damage if caught in melee at 1 tile.
